    B. SCHOLARSHIPS AND INCENTIVES

    B.1 Academic Scholarships

    100% scholarship on tuition for Summa Cum Laude enrolled as first Year
    75% scholarship on tuition for Magna Cum Laude enrolled as first year
    50% scholarship on tuition for Cum Laude enrolled as first year

    a.)  100% scholarship on tuition can be enjoyed on the succeeding
    semester if the recipient has no grade below 1.2;
    b.)   75% scholarship on tuition can be enjoyed if the
    recipient has no  grade below 1.4;
    c.)   50% scholarship on tuition can be enjoyed if the recipient has
    no grade below 1.6; and
    d.)  The recipient has no old accounts

    B.2 Bro. Chamberland Scholarship

    30% scholarship on tuition for employees from public
    and private schools/institutions, employees of CJC, and
    religious men and women

    Regulations

    a.)  To maintain the 30% scholarship on tuition, the recipient
    must have no grade below 1.2
    b.)  The recipient must have no accounts

    B.3 Discounts

    a.)  5% discount on tuition for full payment of both the tuition and miscellaneous fees
    b.)  15% discount on tuition for graduates from CJC College Department   who will enroll in the Graduate School on the first semester of the first school year .

    Regulations

    a.)  The 5% discount can be enjoyed every time a student pays in full his/her tuition and miscellaneous fees
    b.) The 15% discount can only be enjoyed on the first semester of the first school year

    General Policy

    A student can only enjoy one scholarship or discount at
    a time in a semester by choosing whichever is higher.
