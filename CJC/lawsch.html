    SCHOLARSHIPS AND INCENTIVES

    B.1 Academic Scholarship

    100% on tuition for Summa Cum Laude enrolled as first year

    75% on tuition for Magna Cum Laude enrolled as first year 50% on tuition for Cum Laude enrolled as first year

    Regulations

    To maintain the scholarship, the recipient must have the following  General Weighted Average:

    a.)    91% but no grade below 85% to receive 100% scholarship on tuition; b.)    87% but no grade below 83% to receive 75% scholarship on tuition; c.) 85% but no grade below 80% to receive 50% scholarship on Tuition;

    B.2 Bro. Elric Scholarship

    15% on tuition for CJC Alumni and CJC employees enrolled as first year

    Regulations

    The Grant can only be enjoyed on the first semester of the first year

    B.3 Fr. Andre Coindre Scholarship:

    100% scholarship on tuition for students with the highest

    General Weighted Average per year level 50%  scholarship on tuition for students with the second highest General Weighted Average per year level

    Regulations

    To maintain the Fr. Coindre scholarship, the recipient/s

    a.)  Must have no grade below 85%; b.)  Must have taken the full load; c.)  Must have no dropped subject/s; and d.) The scholarship is only granted when the population of the year level is 30 and above

    B.4 Discounts

    5% discount on tuition for full payment of both the tuition and
    miscellaneous fees

    Regulations

    The student can avail of the 5% discount every time the student pays in full his/her tuition and miscellaneous  fees

    B.5 Incentives for BAR Exams Topnotchers

    P150,000 cash for top 1 in the BAR exams
    P100,00 cash for top 2  in the BAR exams
    P75,000 cash for top 3  in the BAR exams
    P50,000 cash for top 4 to 10  in the BAR exams

    Regulations

    a.) The student must have studied at Cor Jesu College for at least one school year.
    b.) The student must have graduated from Cor Jesu College

    General Policy

    A student can only enjoy one scholarship or discount at
    a time in a semester by choosing whichever is higher.
