Graduate School

 Admission Requirements:

 Graduate School
  • Admission Requirements:
    • Graduate of any degree (at least 18 units of education subjects for MAEd)
    • Transcript of Records
    • Honorable Dismissal from last school attended
    • 2 copies passport size picture
    • Accomplished Admission Form (available at the Graduate School Office)
    • Entrance Examination (administered by Guidance and Counseling Center, P300 fee)

  • FOR TRANSFEREES
    • Copy of undergraduate and graduate Transcript of Records
    • Graduate credits earned from other Philippine schools will be evaluated by the Dean
    • Copy of undergraduate Transcript of Records (TOR)
    • Copy of the graduate credits earned from previous Philippine school attended to be evaluated by the dean
    • Copy of graduate units earned from foreign schools to be assessed by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED)
    • Copy of the grades of the applicant who is a baccalaureate degree holder from a foreign school to be evaluated by CHED
    • Residence requirement is at least two (2) semesters with at least eighteen (18) units earned in Cor Jesu College Graduate School
    • Result of the Entrance Examinations administered by Cor Jesu College Graduate School
