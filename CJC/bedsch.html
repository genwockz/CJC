    B. SCHOLARSHIPS AND INCENTIVES

    B.1 Academic Scholarship

    100% scholarship on tuition for Elementary Valedictorian

    50% scholarship on tuition for Elementary Salutatorian

    Regulations

    a.) The scholarship is granted to Valedictorians and Salutatorians who are graduates of a school with a population of 50 or more students. It is good for one school year

    B.2 Academic Achievers – Honors

    100% scholarship on tuition for top 1 from first to third year HS.

    50% scholarship on tuition for top 2 from first to third year HS. 25% scholarship on tuition for top 3 from first to third year HS.

    Regulations

    a.) 100% scholarship on tuition if he/she has a general average of 95% or better with no grade below 93% in all subjects including components of the Makabayan

    b.) 50% scholarship on tuition if he/she has a general average of 93% or better with no grade below 91% in all c.) 25% scholarship on tuition if he/she has a general average of 91% or better with no grade below 89% in all subjects including components of the Makabayan

    B.3 Topnotchers of the CJC-BED Scholarship Test

    100% scholarship on tuition for pupils/students who will score 100 to 120 out of 120 items

    50% scholarship on tuition for pupils/students who will score 85 to 99 out of 120 items 25% scholarship on tuition for pupils/students who will score 75 to 84 out of 120 items

    Regulations

    a.) The scholarship is only for tuition and it is only for one school year

    B.4 Venerable Bro Polycarp Incentives

    0% incentive on tuition for TRANFEREES for the following levels for SY 2013-2014:

    Grade 1
Grade 2          20 slots available
Grade 3          14 slots available
Grade 4          14 slots available
Grade 5          18 slots available
Grade 6           5 slots available
Grade 7/HS-1
Grade 8/HS-2     12 slots available
Grade 9/HS-3
Grade 10/HS-4
    Regulations

    a.) The student can enjoy 30% discount on tuition from point of entry level both for elementary and for high school provided that he/she has a final average grade of 85% and above

    b.) The school will only accept transferees for these levels with discounts only for SY 2013-2014 c.) Must have no account d.) Must have good moral character

    B.5 Sports Scholarships

    Scholarship for the following events:

    a.) Basketball (m)

    b.) Volleyball (m/w)

    Regulations

    a.) 100% scholarship on tuition if winner in national sports competition in the prior year

    b.) 50% scholarship on tuition if winner in regional competition in the prior year c.) Must have no grade lower than 80% d.) Must have good moral character e.) Must have no old accounts

    B.6 Band Scholarship

    25% up to 75% tuition fee scholarship

    B.7 Discounts

    a.) 5% discount on tuition for full payment of both tuition and miscellaneous fees

    b.) 20% discount on tuition for the 3rd child. c.) 30% discount on tuition for the 4th child. d.) 40% discount on tuition for the 5th child. e.) 5% discount on tuition for corporations or institutions with 5 to 15 scholars and 7% discount on tuition for those with 16 and above scholars

    C. Corporate and Government Scholarships

    a.) Educational Service Contracting (ESC-EVS)

    b.) Del Monte

    c.) Hedcor

    General Policy

    A student can only enjoy one(1) scholarship or discount at a time in a school year by choosing whichever is higher.
