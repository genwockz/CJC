 A.Trainee Entry Qualifications

 Based on TESDA Training Regulations, trainees or students should possess the following qualifications

 For Automotive Servicing NC II
  • can communicate both oral and written;
  • is physically and mentally fit;
  • has good moral character;
  • can perform basic mathematical computation; and
  • is physically fit and mentally healthy as certified by a Public Health Officer

For Driving NC IIl
  • has good moral character;
  • can communicate both oral and written
  • is physically fit and mentally healthy as certified by a Public Health Officer
For Machining NC II
  • has good moral character;
  • can communicate both oral and written
  • is physically fit and mentally healthy as certified by a Public Health Officer
For Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) NC II
  • has completed training in SMAW NC I, or a holder of SMAW NC I
  • has good moral character;
  • can communicate both oral and written
  • is physically fit and mentally healthy as certified by a Public Health Officer
B. Institutional Requirements

 For New Enrollees
  • High School Card (F 137A if high school graduate)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • ID Picture (4 pcs. 1×1)
  • Barangay/ Police Clearance
  • Birth Certificate (photocopy)
  • Marriage Certificate (if married)
  • NCAE
  • YP4SC
For Transferees
  • Transcript of Records (if applicable)
  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Barangay/ Police Clearance
  • ID Picture (4 pcs. 1×1)
  • Birth Certificate (photocopy)
  • Marriage Certificate (if married)
  • NCAE
  • YP4SC
