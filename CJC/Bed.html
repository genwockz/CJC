Basic Education Department

 Admission Requirements:

 Preschool
  • Kindergarten I
    • At least 4 years old by October
    • Interview by the School Counselor or Registering Officer
    • NSO Birth Certificate (photocopy)
    • 3 pcs. Passport size I.D. picture

  • Kindergarten II (for Transferees)
    • At least 5 years old by October
    • Interview by the School Counselor or Registering Officer
    • NSO Birth Certificate (photocopy)
    • Readiness Test
    • 3 pcs. Passport size I.D. picture

High School
  • Freshmen
    • Report Card
    • Classification Test
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • NSO Birth Certificate (photocopy)
    • ACR and Study Permit of DepEd for Aliens (for Foreign Students only)
    • 3 pcs. Passport size I.D. picture

  • Old Students
    • Report Card
    • Summer Report Card or Certificate of Summer Grade from the Registrar (for students who are academically challenged)
    • Clearance from the Prefect of Discipline
Grade School
  • Grade 1
    • Must be 6 years old by October
    • Assessment/Classification Test
    • Kindergarten Report Card or Certificate
    • NSO Birth Certificate (photocopy)
    • 3 pcs. Passport size I.D. picture

  • Old Pupils
    • Report card
    • Clearance from the Prefect of Discipline
TRANSFEREES (Grade School and High School)
  • Grade 1
    • Report Card
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • NSO Birth Certificate (photocopy)
    • Interview by the Prefect of Discipline or Principal
    • Letter request addressed to the Principal stating the reason for transfer
    • Photocopy of Alien Certificate of Registration (for Foreign Students only)
    • 3 pcs. Passport size I.D. picture
